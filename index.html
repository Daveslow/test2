Day-by-Day Activities

Flight

Day 0: The Departure

Pack your bags for the end of the world. Today is entirely dedicated to travel.

  • Depart Chicago O'Hare (ORD) in the afternoon.
  • Overnight flight to South America (approx 10-12 hours).
  • Tip: Drink plenty of water and try to sleep; you need energy for tomorrow.
Transit

Day 1: Welcome to Chile

Land in Santiago and immediately head south to Patagonia.

  • Land in Santiago (SCL).
  • Connect to domestic flight to Puerto Natales.
  • Check into your hostel/hotel and rent trekking gear (poles, stove, sleeping bag).
  • Attend the mandatory W Trek Briefing.
The W Trek

Days 2-6: Torres del Paine

5 days of world-class backpacking through the granite spires.

  • Hike: The iconic Las Torres (The Towers) viewpoint.
  • Explore: The hanging glaciers of the French Valley.
  • Marvel: The massive blue wall of Glacier Grey.
  • Logistics: Book your campsites via Vertice (West side) and Las Torres (East side).
Road Trip

Day 7: The Road to Sunset

Transfer to Argentina and start the legendary drive north on Ruta 40. We've added 3 "off the beaten path" stops to break up the drive.

  • 08:00 AM: Bus from Puerto Natales to El Calafate (5 hours).
  • 02:00 PM: Pick up rental car in El Calafate.

1. Punta Walichu (3:30 PM)

Ancient cave paintings right on the shore of Lake Argentino. A quiet archeological site most tourists miss. Read More

2. Parador La Leona (5:30 PM)

Historic roadhouse where Butch Cassidy hid out. Mandatory stop for coffee and their famous lemon pie. Visit Site

3. Mirador Viedma (7:30 PM)

The "Instagram Spot." As you round the lake, Mt. Fitz Roy explodes into view. Perfect lighting in the evening.

  • 09:30 PM: Arrive in El Chaltén for a late dinner and sunset (it stays light until 10 PM!).
Hiking

Day 8: Mount Fitz Roy

The trekking capital of Argentina offers its crown jewel today.

  • Hike to Laguna de los Tres (20km round trip).
  • Eat lunch at the cobalt blue alpine lake.
  • Return to town for craft beer at La Cervecería (Try the Bock!).
Activity Choice

Day 9: Vertical or Chill

Choose your own adventure day.

  • Option A (Adrenaline): Multi-pitch rock climbing at Paredón de los Cóndores.
  • Option B (Relaxed): Drive the gravel road to Lago del Desierto.
  • Visit Chorrillo del Salto waterfall.
Glacier

Day 10: Perito Moreno & Fly Out

See one of the few advancing glaciers in the world.

  • Drive back to El Calafate (3 hours).
  • Walk the boardwalks of Perito Moreno Glacier.
  • Listen to the "white thunder" of ice calving.
  • Fly from El Calafate (FTE) to Buenos Aires (AEP).
City & Culture

Days 11-12: The Paris of South America

Decompress with red wine, steak, and tango.

  • Walk the colorful streets of La Boca (Caminito).
  • Visit the tomb of Evita Perón at Recoleta Cemetery.
  • Eat the world's best steak at Don Julio (Book 2 months ahead!).
  • Catch a late-night flight back to Chicago on Day 12.
Home

Day 13: Welcome Home

  • Arrive in Chicago (ORD) early morning.
  • You survived Patagonia!
